2.教学horse
Listen:who’s coming(马叫声)
S：马
教读horse强调or的发音。Read more words with or.

接着呈现图片引出复数horses。What are thoseThey are horses.
看图说话They are______horses.(tall/short/thin/fat/strong/cute)
3.Sheep
出示图片：Is it a horseNo,it isn’t. It’s a sheep.从meet see等单词中教读ee在sheep中发[ i：]。
Let’s count：one sheep. Two sheep.Three sheep....
引导学生自己观察总结出sheep单数和复数相同,sheep的复数还是sheep.
These are sheep.They are so cute.
