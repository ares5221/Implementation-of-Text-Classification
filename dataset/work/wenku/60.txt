德育案例教学设计
单位：利津县明集乡中心学校
姓名：刘艳霞
Unit 4 Don’t eat in class.

课题：Rules
教学目标:
1.否定祈使句?(Don’t…)
Don’t eat in class.
You can eat in the dining hall, but we can’t eat in class.
I can’t go out on school nights.
2.能使用情态动词can表示许可?(Can?for permission)
A: Can we wear a hat in school
B: Yes, we can. / No, we can’t.
3.能正确使用have to和must谈论规章制度?(Modal verbs?have to, must)
A: Does he have to wear a uniform at school
B: Yes, he does. / No, he doesn’t.
We must be on time for class.
教学策略:
1.能根据常识预测听力内容
2.根据文章特点和活动要求使用有效学习策略
德育目标：
了解中外相同或不同的校规﹑班规及家规，理解认识民主、文明、平等、公正、法制等社会主义核心价值观。
教学步骤
Step 1 Free Talk
Have a chant with the Ss.
Talk about school rules.
Show the students some pictures.
Play a game with the Ss, let them guess “What’s the home rule”
设计意图：通过“说唱”和“猜规则”活动，让学生明白制定民主、平等、法制规则的重要性。并为听力训练做好铺垫。
Step 2 Listening
Listen to Dave’s rules and fill in the blanks.
设计意图：把主要情态动词can, can’t, has to, must和新学短语及时间设空，提高学生的听力理解能力。
Step 3Reading
1. SkimmingReading
Read the passage as quickly as you can, pay more attention to?the first sentence and the last sentence.
Answer the question:?How does Molly feel about the rules
2. Scanning reading
Go through the passage quickly, pay attention to?the sentences with key words “Don’t”, “can’t”, “must”, “have to”.
Read the letters,?underline?the rules for Molly.
设计意图：通过介绍Skimming Reading，Scanning Reading等阅读方法，帮助学生快速捕捉信息，完成阅读任务。通过图表帮助学生明白文章的框架结构，并为信息输出、写作做好准备。
Step 4 Group Discussion
Work in groups, discuss Molly’s rules.
(1) Which rules do you think are fair(公平)
(2) Which rules do you think are unfair(不公平)
(3) Why
Fill in the blanks with (√), (×). And try to answer?Why.
设计意图：让学生多感官接受相关信息，对分离出的信息进行反思及再判断，发表自己的看法，形成正确的社会主义核心价值观点：公平、公正。
Step 5Reading
StudyingReading
Read the passage more slowly and carefully.
Read Dr. Know’s letter to Molly. Try to find out Dr. Know’s idea about rules. Then fill in the blanks with right words.?
设计意图：运用研读法，找准细节性信息。帮助学生挖掘文章深层次含义，理清文章的脉络结构。阅读中感悟平等、公正、法制的重要性和必要性。
Step 6 Group Work
Discuss in groups:
Nothing can be done without rules. (没有规矩，不成方圆)
After watching the pictures, think about: What can we do
People can’t break the rules. We must call on them to follow the rules.
设计意图：通过图片展示、小组讨论，让学生明白Rules的重要性，对学生进行情感教育。参考图标，号召人们遵守各种规章制度，为写作做好准备。
Step 7 Writing
Make a poster. Write cool rules for all the students. Call on them to be good children(文明少年). The title may be “To be good children”.
设计意图：通过制作海报，整合Section A部分学习的School Rules，Section B所学的Home Rules，并拓展到其他公共场合的Public Rules。
Step 8 Summary
设计意图：让学生自己总结当堂收获，不断反思，不断提高。学习语言知识的同时，加强学生的德育教育。
Homework：Make some?Class?Rules?with your classmates.
